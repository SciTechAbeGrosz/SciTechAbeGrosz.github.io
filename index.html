Rubix Cube Beginner Tutorial

Overview

In the beginner's method for solving the Rubix Cube, the white cross is solved, then the rest of the white side (and in doing so, solving the bottom layer, which I'll talk about later), then the middle layer, then the yellow side, and finally the top layer. The top layer is going to be the yellow side if the white cross is solved instead of another color cross. For example, if the blue cross was done first, then the green side would be the top layer, since blue and green are always opposite sides. Speaking of which...
